Project 1

This is a Pre Motorcycle Ride Check List Program.

This Check List Program will walk a rider through the basic check list every rider should inspect before going on a bike ride.

The Pre Motorcycle Ride Check List will guide a rider through the inspection of motor oils, hydraulic brake fluids, tire pressure, and last but not least, the electrical system. The electrical system involves inspecting the head lights, turn signals, and license plate lights.
